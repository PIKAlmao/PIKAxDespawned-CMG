‚Ä¢     Initiation lasts 10 minutes.

‚Ä¢     In order to have valid initiation, you must meet the following criteria:

* &#x20;      Have a visible weapon in your hands.

‚Ä¢     If someone attempts to damage you with a weapon or vehicle with malicious intent, you may shoot back & defend yourself without being prosecuted for RDM. You may report the person who damages you whether you die or win in your self-defence.

‚Ä¢     Seeing another player open their vehicle boot or inventory is not a valid reason to initiate.

‚Ä¢     Seeing two players pass items between each other is not a valid reason to initiate.

‚Ä¢     Initiating at a traffic stop or at a border force location is not allowed.
